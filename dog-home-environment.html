Safety and Security

Dogs are pack animals and they feel happy when they are with their family. Dogs like things a little different from people, though, and I'll tell you how.

Dogs need a comfortable, dry place to rest. They also need a few places to hide. They like wide open areas with lots of trees to pee on. If you have more than one dog, like me, than take this advice: you MUST keep them from fighting. Trust me, I've seen it, it's not pretty.

Dogs can overheat, quickly. They have thick fur coats and cannot sweat to cool off. Leaving a dog in a hot car is illegal in some places. It should be illegal everywhere. The temperature in a closed car can quickly rise 30 or more degrees higher than the outside air. If it's 90 degrees outside, that's deadly!

On an 85-degree day, for example, the temperature inside a car with the windows opened slightly can reach 102 degrees within 10 minutes. After 30 minutes, the temperature will reach 120 degrees. humanesociety.org

Checklist
