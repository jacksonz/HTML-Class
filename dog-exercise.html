Canine Fit

What's the best part about a dog that needs frequent runs? It'll make YOU live longer. So put on those running shoes and hit sidewalks.

The best way to run your dog is to run to your favorite coffee shop or restaurant. You can also throw a Frisbee or a ball at the park.

If your dog doesn't get enough exercise, they'll be the naughtiest dog in your neighborhood. I'll tell you how:

  • Destructive Chewing and Scratching
  • Garbage Raids
  • Hyper-activity
  • Barking and Whining
  • Nipping and Biting

Some ways to exercise your dog that are easy for you are:

  • Fetch
  • Bubble Catching
  • Food Maze
  • Rope Toys
  • Training for Agility

Don't forget to exercise your dog's brain, too. They like to play games such as hiding their food or hide and seek.
